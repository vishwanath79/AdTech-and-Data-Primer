# AdTech Grounding Principles and Terms

## Preface

Adtech (advertising technology) refers to tools that help manage online advertising campaigns. As you read this book, you will encounter various terms and concepts related to adtech.

Here are some key concepts to understand:

- Martech: Adtech is a subset of martech (marketing technology) specifically for paid advertising.
- Programmatic Advertising: Adtech makes programmatic advertising possible, which uses AI to automate ad buying and selling.
