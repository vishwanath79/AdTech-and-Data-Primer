References#

This section lists the key resources, documentation, and publications referenced throughout this book.

Industry Standards & Documentation#

  • OpenRTB Specification by Interactive Advertising Bureau (2024). Standard protocol for programmatic advertising communication.

  • IAB Standards & Guidelines by Interactive Advertising Bureau (2024). Industry standards for ad formats, measurement, and viewability.

  • Google Ad Manager API Documentation by Google (2024). Comprehensive API documentation for programmatic ad serving.

  • Facebook Marketing API by Meta (2024). Documentation for Facebook/Meta advertising APIs.

  • Prebid.js by Prebid.org (2024). Open-source header bidding wrapper.

Books#

  • Programmatic Advertising: The Successful Transformation to Automated Digital Advertising by Oliver Busch (2016). Springer. ISBN: 978-3319250212.

  • Data-Driven Marketing: The 15 Metrics Everyone in Marketing Should Know by Mark Jeffery (2010). Wiley. ISBN: 978-0470504543.

  • Trustworthy Online Controlled Experiments: A Practical Guide to A/B Testing by Ron Kohavi, Diane Tang, and Ya Xu (2020). Cambridge University Press. ISBN: 978-1108724265.

  • Designing Data-Intensive Applications: The Big Ideas Behind Reliable, Scalable, and Maintainable Systems by Martin Kleppmann (2017). O’Reilly Media. ISBN: 978-1449373320.

Publications & News#

  • Digiday - Media and marketing news and perspective.
